FAQ

Frequently asked
questions

How does the free trial work?

Start with our free Starter plan — no credit card required. When you're ready to unlock more power, upgrade to Pro with a 14-day free trial. Cancel anytime during the trial and you won't be charged.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll only be charged the prorated difference. When downgrading, the remaining balance is credited to your account.

What happens if I exceed my execution limit?

We'll never stop your workflows. If you go over your limit, we'll send a friendly heads-up and give you a 7-day grace period to upgrade or optimize your workflows. No surprise charges.

Is my data secure?

Security is our top priority. We're SOC 2 Type II certified, encrypt all data at rest and in transit with AES-256, and support SSO with SAML 2.0. We also offer on-premise deployment for enterprise customers.

Do you offer discounts for startups?

Yes! We offer 50% off for the first year for startups with under $5M in funding. Contact our sales team with proof of eligibility and we'll get you set up within 24 hours.

Can I use FlowSync with my existing CI/CD pipeline?

FlowSync integrates natively with GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, CircleCI, Jenkins, and more. You can trigger workflows from your CI/CD pipeline or vice versa using our REST API or webhooks.
